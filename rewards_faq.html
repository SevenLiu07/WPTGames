What is WPT Rewards?

WPT Rewards is an exciting player rewards program that gives you exclusive benefits as you accumulate Reward Points and redeem prizes from our Rewards Store. This is our way of thanking you for your loyalty and support.


Reward Points is the virtual currency for WPT Rewards. You can use your Reward Points to buy Gold packages to play more or save it for prizes and discounts in the future as we build our Reward Store.


Reward Points have no cash value so cannot be cashed out. Reward Points are specific to each account and are not transferable.


You can get Reward Points by playing in high stake ring games on PlayWPT Poker. The more hands you play and the bigger the stakes, the more XPs (or experience points) you earn and the faster you advance to the next Level. Your Level determines your Reward Points Multiplier, helping you earn Reward Points faster.

Elite Status

* XP penalty is triggered after Level 10. If you do not reach a new level within a month, you will lose XPs based on the XP Penalty of that Level.

After completing Level 17, you will be granted Elite status and earn 40X Reward Points! However, to retain your Elite status, you will have to complete monthly and annual XP requirements.

LevelReward Points MultiplierMonthly XPAnnual XP
Elite Status40X300,0002,800,000

As an Elite member, you will need to earn 300,000 XPs every month. If you fail to complete this requirement, you will lose an XP Life. You will be given 3 XP Lives. If you lose all your XP Lives, then you will drop to Level 17.


Secondly, you will need to earn 2.8 million XPs every year. If you missed this target, your Elite status will be downgraded to Level 17.
